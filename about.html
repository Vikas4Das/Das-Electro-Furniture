About Das Electro Furniture

Blending functionality with aesthetics to transform your home into a masterpiece.

Founded with the vision of redefining modern furniture shopping in India, Das Electro Furniture began as a humble idea among three passionate creators. With an aim to provide stylish, durable, and affordable furniture for every space, we grew from a small local workshop into a thriving e-commerce platform serving homes nationwide.

Bhola Nath Das

Founder & CEO

Bhola Nath Das is a visionary entrepreneur with a background in industrial design and retail management. He turned his love for minimalist living and tech-forward retail into a premium furniture brand. With over 10 years of experience, he leads the company with innovation and customer-centric design.

Vikas Das

Co-Founder & Creative Director

Vikas is the creative mind behind our product aesthetics and brand image. With her degree in interior architecture and a flair for visual storytelling, she ensures every piece we deliver complements modern Indian homes with elegance and purpose.

Pankja Das

Co-Founder & Operations Head

Pankaj manages the operational excellence of the business, from supply chain logistics to vendor partnerships. With a background in manufacturing and business development, his efficiency keeps the engine of Das Electro running smoothly and sustainably.

How We Started

  1. Research & Ideation: Identified a gap in the Indian furniture market for affordable modern design.
  2. Prototyping: Created custom-built furniture pieces in a small workshop.
  3. Local Launch: Started selling through local exhibitions and word-of-mouth.
  4. Online Platform: Developed our website with seamless UX and mobile-first design.
  5. Nationwide Reach: Expanded logistics, optimized packaging, and partnered with local artisans.
  6. Future Vision: Planning eco-friendly collections and smart furniture integrations.
